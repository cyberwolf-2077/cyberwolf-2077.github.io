Context

I went to leHACK 2023 and got the chance to participate to the hardware village workshop, ending up with a board loaded with 4 LEDs that can be turned on when powered.
This is cool but somewhat basic.
When I went home, I found some chips laying around on my desk and ended up thinking of a little project that would use a NE555N chip to make the LEDs blink
